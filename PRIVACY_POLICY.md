# Privacy Policy for Chroma Pour

**Last Updated: February 24, 2026**

Chroma Pour ("the App") is developed as a free-to-play game. This PRIVACY POLICY is intended to inform you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for anyone using our App.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**Chroma Pour does not collect, store, or share any personal identity data.**
*   The game is a Progressive Web App (PWA).
*   Any game progress (levels, settings) is stored locally on your device using `localStorage`.
*   We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking software.

## 2. Cookies
Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. The App may use "cookies" (or similar local storage technology) only to save your current level and game state for a better user experience.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App may link to third-party services (like GitHub Pages for hosting). These external sites are not operated by us. Therefore, we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party services.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer.
